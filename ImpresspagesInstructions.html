Impresspages Instructions 

Installing Funnelll on ImpressPages is very simple! Just follow these instructions!

Step 1: Open menu, and select design.

Step 2: Click on Edit HTML/CSS

Step 3: Select Header.php

Step 4: Add the Funnelll code snippet provided as high up in the header as you can.

Step 5: Visit the Funnelll site, and you should be good to go!
